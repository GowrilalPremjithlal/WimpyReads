About the Author

Jeff Kinney is the warm, creative spirit behind the Diary of a Wimpy Kid series—a storyteller who turns the everyday mishaps of school life into an international phenomenon. From his early days as a kid doodling in the margins of his notebooks, Jeff discovered a knack for capturing those awkward, hilarious moments that everyone can relate to. His quirky cartoons and playful observations quickly evolved into the beloved stories of Greg Heffley, which have resonated with millions of readers around the world.

What makes Jeff truly special is his uncanny ability to find humor and heart in the ordinary. His writing is like a friendly conversation, filled with the kind of wit and honesty that makes you feel understood—whether you’re laughing at the trials of middle school or reminiscing about your own youth. Jeff’s work reminds us that life’s little imperfections are what make our stories unique and worth telling.

Beyond his role as an author, Jeff is known for his genuine, approachable personality and his commitment to inspiring creativity in young readers. He’s not just a writer; he’s a champion of finding joy in the simple, everyday moments, encouraging everyone to see the beauty and laughter in life’s quirks. His journey from a passionate cartoonist to a best-selling author is a testament to the idea that even the smallest doodle can spark big smiles and leave a lasting impact.
